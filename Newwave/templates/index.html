HOW NEW WAVE WORKS?

New Wave is a roleplay platform designed to reduce the gap between "studying language" and "understand and speak".

Our team strongly believe that fluency builds through experience.

Forget boring memorization! Dive into real-world simulations where your mission is to complete all the trials!

WHY OUR METHODS ARE EFFECTIVE?

People learn languages better when they need to find a "key" to unlock other scenarios.

Instead of memorizing a list of words, our users are going through digital missions.

Immersive Scenarios open the "Flow State".

Motivating

Games stimulate motivation to learn more, and students become very absorbed in competitive aspects of them. Moreover, learners try harder than in traditional courses.

Reducing stress

Students have to face unfamiliar grammatical structures, words, passages that lead to a highest level of stress. Since people understand that in roleplays they may make mistakes...

Adaptive&Relevant

Games provide language practice in various skills - writing, listening and reading. They create a meaningful context for the use of languages such as simple talks, relevant phrases, memes.
